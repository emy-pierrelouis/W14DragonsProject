Terms and Conditions

1. Estimated Availability:

  • Common dragons: Estimated delivery within 1-2 weeks.
  • Uncommon dragons: Estimated delivery within 2-6 weeks.
  • Rare dragons: Estimated delivery within 3-4 months.
  • Limited dragons: We will contact you when they become available.
  • Protected dragons: Delivery may range from several weeks to nearly a year.
  • Seasonal dragons: Availability aligns with their breeding seasons; e.g., Northern dragons exclusively available during winter, while some seasonal dragons may be accessible for up to two months.

2. Documentation and Permits:

At the time of pickup, the customer must possess all appropriate documentation, including:

  • Standard Dragon Ownership License (mandatory).
  • Advanced Dragon Ownership License (mandatory if purchasing seasonal, rare, and limited dragons requiring experience).
  • Protected Dragon Ownership License (mandatory if purchasing protected dragon breeds).
  • Habitat Inspection Report.
  • Dragon Transport Permit.
  • Proof of Residence.
  • Dragon Care and Handling Proficiency Certification (as required for specific dragon breeds).
  • Criminal Background Clearance for Protected Dragon Ownership.
  • Specialized Dragon Occupation Certification (for those purchasing dragon breeds related to their job occupation).

3. Company Liability:

Ignis Aeternus Company is committed to sourcing the right dragon egg for our customers and ensuring the dragons' well-being during our care and transportation. We are responsible for the dragon's health and safety up until the point of pickup.

4. Deposit and Payment:

  • Customers are required to pay a deposit of 1/3 of the dragon egg's price upon processing their form.
  • This deposit is refundable only if the dragon does not imprint with the customer.
  • he deposit will not be returned if the customer chooses to wait for another available dragon egg.
  • The remaining 2/3 of the payment is due upon the pickup of the dragon egg.

5. Pickup Location:

Dragon egg pickup is exclusively available at designated Ignis Aeternus Company locations.

6. Ownership Responsibility:

While Ignis Aeternus Company takes utmost care in providing healthy and ethically sourced dragons, the responsibility for the dragon's health and well-being transfers to the owner upon pickup.

We offer guidance and resources to support proper care but cannot be held liable for conditions arising after the sale.

7. Insurance and Warranties:

Ignis Aeternus Company does not currently offer insurance or warranties for dragons purchased from our company.

We encourage owners to explore third-party insurance options and thoroughly understand any policies they may consider.
