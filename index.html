Igniting Dreams, One Dragon at a Time

At Ignis Aeternus Company, we extend a warm and illustrious welcome to you, our esteemed visitor, as you step into the enchanting realm of dragons. As guardians of these magnificent creatures and torchbearers of responsible dragon ownership, we are thrilled to introduce you to our world, where wonder, wisdom, and ethical practices converge.

Our Legacy: Founded by Elyssandra Ravensong, a former dragon rider and explorer, Ignis Aeternus has been a trusted presence in the dragon community for several years. Elyssandra's unwavering commitment to the welfare of dragons, borne from her own personal journey and a deep sense of responsibility, is the bedrock upon which our company is built.

A World of Opportunities

Here, with our company, you will discover a wealth of opportunities and resources designed to fulfill your dragon-related aspirations. Whether you're seeking to bring a dragon into your life, deepen your understanding of these mythical creatures, or simply explore the mystical world they inhabit, Ignis Aeternus stands ready to guide you.

Ethical Sourcing

Our commitment to ethical practices is unyielding. We source dragon eggs exclusively from trusted and ethical dragon breeding sanctuaries. Each egg you find here carries with it the promise of responsible breeding and a bright future for both dragon and owner.

Comprehensive Services

Ignis Aeternus is more than just a source of dragon eggs. We offer a suite of services to support you on your dragon journey, including access to renowned veterinarian services, expert guidance on dragon care and handling, and immersive courses to elevate your dragon expertise.

A Commitment to Excellence

Our dedication to excellence infuses every facet of our work. It drives us to maintain the highest standards of quality, transparency, and ethical conduct, ensuring that every dragon and dragon owner finds a safe haven with Ignis Aeternus.
